Who we are and what we do

We are a software development group from the Industrial University of Santander, Colombia. We are focused on the development of web applications and informatic solutions to help the University schools to manage their information and join the University community using cutting edge technologies.

The professor Luis Ignacio González Ramírez from the Industrial University of Santander has been leading the group since 2003. Furthermore, our projects have been developed by students who belong to the Systems Engineering school. Nowadays, there are some engineers and students who are working to improve the services we offer.
