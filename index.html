Who we are and what we do

We are a software development group from the Universidad Industrial de Santander, Colombia. We are focused on the development of web applications and informatic solutions to help the University schools to manage their information and join the University community using cutting edge technologies.

The professor Luis Ignacio González Ramírez from the Industrial University of Santander has been leading the group since 2003. Furthermore, our projects have been developed by students who belong to the Systems Engineering school. Nowadays, there are some engineers and students who are working to improve the services we offer.

Projects

We work in some projects, there are some open source, but most of them are focused in our main project, COMA. Our leading projects are:

COMA [Private]

Web framework with applications for academic communities.

RealTime

Real-time communication applications system for COMA sub-projects.

Elise Library [Private]

Frontend library to create elegant powerful applications extending anothers.

Elise Library [Beta]

Frontend library for agile elegant and responsive development.

¿Who are using our projects?

There are some schools, faculties and organizations who are starting to use our portal too.
