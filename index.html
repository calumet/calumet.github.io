Who we are and what we do?

We are a software developer group at the Industrial University of Santander, Colombia. We focus on develop web applications and informatic solutions to help to manage corporate information and communicate the university community using cutting edge technologies.

In charge of the teacher Luis Ignacio González Ramírez, the group has grown up since 2003 by students works. Nowadays, there are some engineers and students working to improve the services we offer.
