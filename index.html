Who we are and what we do

We are a software developer group at the Industrial University of Santander, Colombia. We focus on develop web applications and informatic solutions to help to manage University schools information and join the University community using cutting edge technologies.

The professor Luis Ignacio González Ramírez at the Industrial University of Santander is leading the group Calumet since 2003. Furthermore, the software projects have been developed by students who belong at the Systems Engineering school. Nowadays, there are some engineers and students who are working to improve the services we offer.
